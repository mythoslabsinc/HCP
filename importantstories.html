Important Stories

As a part of the journey to combat harmful online content, it is important to see the stories of those impacted by it, along with those who have dedicated their lives to combating harmful content and hate in all its forms. Here are a few for you to experience:

Falling Into Hate: Stories of Regret

Being surrounded by harmful content and ideologies can lead you to do things that negatively impact others and society for decades to come, and cause you to live a lifetime of regret. Four individuals who fell into that cycle speak about how their actions, regrets and growth.

The Heart of Discrimination

Caste-based discrimination in India has had devastating consequences on the nation and its people for generations. Suraj Yengde speaks about the need for urgent prevention of caste-based hate and violence.

Striving for Coexistence

Learning to see others as equals and working together to build a healthier, happier society is at the heart of countering harmful and hateful content. Madan Gopal Singh delves into the shallow nature of harmful content and the power of coexistence.
