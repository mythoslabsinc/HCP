Countering Harmful Content

Understanding how to protect yourself from harmful content and those who spread it is an important skill for anyone using social media. Here are some steps you can follow to identify, understand and defend against online hate speech:

Understand the Impact

Harmful content can affect how you think and feel, leading to negativity and prejudice. It can harm your relationships and well-being.

Question What You See

Be cautious of what you come across online. Check information from reliable sources before believing or sharing it. Avoid falling for false stories or wrong information.

Look for Different Perspectives

Seek out diverse opinions from trusted sources. Don't get stuck in one viewpoint. Listening to different perspectives helps you understand things better.

Report and Block

Take action against harmful content by reporting it to the platform. Block people who spread hate or negativity to protect yourself.

Spread Positivity

Share uplifting and positive content. Support messages that encourage understanding, empathy, and unity. Let's create a kind and inclusive online environment.

Take Care of Yourself

Look after your mental health by setting limits on social media. Take breaks when needed and focus on activities that make you happy in the real world.
